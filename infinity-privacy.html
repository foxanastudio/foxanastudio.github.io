Infinity (iOS) Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

This app is provided by Foxana Studio at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page is used to inform users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of information if anyone decides to use our Service.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We do not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Log Data

In case of an error in the app, we may collect technical data (“Log Data”). This may include your device model, iOS version, app version, time and date of the error, and diagnostic information. This information is used strictly for debugging and improving the app. None of this data personally identifies you.

Subscriptions

The app offers auto-renewable subscriptions processed through Apple’s App Store using StoreKit 2. All payments are handled securely by Apple. We do not collect, process, or store your payment information.

We may receive limited, non-personally identifiable information related to your subscription status (such as active or expired status) in order to provide access to subscription features within the app.

For more information about how Apple handles your data, please review Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services & APIs

The app uses public APIs from third-party services, including Imgur, Gfycat, and Streamable, etc. When you use features that access these services, content may be displayed directly in the app or you may be directed to the third-party service.

These external services are not operated by us. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content, privacy policies, or practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store certain information locally on your device to function properly. This may include:

• App settings and preferences
• Account authentication tokens used to access Reddit services on your behalf, stored securely in the iOS Keychain (no passwords are collected or stored by us)
• Subscriptions, favorites, or saved content
• Cached media and browsing history

This data is stored locally and is not transmitted to us unless explicitly required for app functionality.

Permissions

The app may request certain iOS permissions when necessary, such as:

• Access to Photos (to save or upload media)
• Notifications (to notify you of updates or activity)
• Biometric authentication (if enabled for security features)
• Network access (required for core app functionality)

Permissions are only requested when required and can be managed in your device settings.

Security

We value your trust in using our app and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
